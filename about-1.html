About

b. 1986, HK.

Hi, my name is Alexandru Negoita, I am a concept artist, illustrator, and all-around creative person with 15 years of serving the video games industry. I love creating fantasy art and exotic worlds, interested in video games and visual aesthetics.
I love role-playing games, storytelling, and world-building, so back in 2019 I have co-created Shardstone Assembly and published Scavenger, a D&D campaign sourcebook, and art book fully illustrated by me.
My illustration work has been selected to be featured in Spectrum: The Best in Contemporary Fantastic Art in 2017 and 2019, also in various issues of Exposé and Imagine FX.

Clients: Lava Potion, New Blood Interactive, hunters entertainment, Deviant Art, Elvellon, King, EA, Gameloft
